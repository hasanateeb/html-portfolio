Ateeb's Favourite Movies

This is a website to highlight some of the favourite movies of Mr. Syed Ateeb Ul Hasan

Movie Names

Dilwale Dhulania Le Jayenge

Summary

Raj and Simran meet during a trip across Europe and the two fall in love.
However, when Raj learns that Simran is already promised to another, he follows her to India to win her and her father over.

Mohabbatein

Summary

Six sprightly, vivacious young students come to study in his Gurukul. They do the forbidden. They fall in love. The patriarch is outraged. The lovers feel helpless.
Gradually they start accepting that their relationship was probably doomed from the start. Right then steps in their saviour in the form of a music teacher.

Veer Zaara

Summary

Squadron Leader Veer Pratap Singh, a pilot in the Indian air force, rescues the stranded Zaara, a woman from Pakistan, following a bus accident, and their lives are forever bound.
With all the ingredients of a classic love story, the film is a tale of love, separation, courage and sacrifice that unfolds over 22 years.
